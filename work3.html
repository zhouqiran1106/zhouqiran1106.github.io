Requirement & Purpose

We hope to integrate medical resources in the internal and external of the hospital through our design and reduce the differences of medical resource allocation in different regions in China. Nowadays, too many people in China go to hospital but they don’t have serious problems. The distribution of medical resources among hospitals in emergencies such as night is uneven. Through the research and prototyping, a complete system consisting of the hospital end and patient end will be presented. Research methods include: The stakeholder analysis chart, The customer experience process chart, Brainstorming image organization flow chart, Literature review Focus group, The contextual inquiry.
