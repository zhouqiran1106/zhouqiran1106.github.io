BEE GARDEN

Basic information

Type: Interactive game
Software: Processing PS AI AU PR
Handware: Kinect
Team member:4


Requirement & Purpose

This is a child insect knowledge science as the goal, designed for children a motion-sensing interactive game. Our aim is to combine education with fun, so that children can fully mobilize their limbs to gather honey and brew bee honey together with some small bees in the interactive game of somatosensory interaction. Learn about bees in the game.



Basic information

Female worker bee Detective bee Male worker bee

1. The player acts as a scout. Male and female worker bees are partners to help players collect nectar from flowers.
2. Scout bees find the source of honey and then call their partners to help collect the nectar.
3. The detective bee will make bee when collect the nectar is over.
