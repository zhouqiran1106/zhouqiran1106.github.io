GUNDAM ROBOT

Basic information

Name: Gundam Robot
Software: Processing
Type: Gnertive design Enterrtion design
Team Member: 1


Requirement & Purpose

This work is an attempt to study computer art for the first time. Through the writing of code, it tries to endow human performance behavior to the robot. To perform simple interactions such as blinking. At the same time through the robot code to consolidate the classroom knowledge, complete their first generative art works.



The main interaction about the robot

1. There is a jet port belching particles under the feet to indicate that the robot is flying upward.
2. Your eyes blink.
3, the wings after experiencing a second or so of acceleration to maintain a constant speed flap, and flight.
4. Turn your right arm slightly as if greeting you.
5. The energy body gathers at the top of the head, making the robot look less rigid and slightly more interesting.
6. The background velocity line indicates the rising speed (which corresponds to the speed line changing rapidly when accelerating below).
7. There is a uniform rotation of the energy body on the chest. When the energy body is fully charged, the green light will flash in the chest. At this time, press the right mouse button energy will contract, after a few seconds of energy storage will be ejected by the white main laser and six yellow secondary laser beam. Laser light leaves traces on the screen. At the same time, with the increase of time, the laser light energy will gradually reduce, white main laser gradually thinner. When the white main laser becomes the thinnest, the energy is insufficient. At this time, the green light on the chest will turn to red light to indicate the need for charging. At this time, release the right mouse button to contract the energy will spread out to charge. When full, the red light on the chest will turn green to indicate that the next laser can be fired.
8. The energy in front of the mouse wheel will gradually grow to form a protective cover.
9. Press the left mouse button to open accelerated flight mode. The back will shoot out a lot of particles like thrusters. The energy gathered overhead scatters left and right to represent the compressed air flow at high speed. The ball of energy on the left shoulder and the right shoulder starts to gather energy and emit a white light to show that the robot is trying to maintain the stability of the entire robot structure during wind pressure. The tips of the wings change from grey to light blue and emit energy. At the same time, the velocity line behind him will change rapidly, indicating an increase in speed.
10. The energy of the left shoulder and the right shoulder will follow the mouse to rotate in the shoulder in normal time, increasing the interest of interaction.
11. The small robot under the left foot of the big robot will rely on the big robot foot under normal circumstances. But when faced with danger, the direction key of the control keyboard can control the movement of the small robot. At this time, the small robot will enter the combat mode, with its body at attention and its head sticking out of the muzzle. Press "a" to shoot down the danger.
